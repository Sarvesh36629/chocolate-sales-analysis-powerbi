# 🍫 Chocolate Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard | Power BI
End-to-end Power BI project analyzing chocolate sales performance using interactive dashboards, DAX, and data modeling to uncover revenue, profit, and business insights.
## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze the sales and profitability of a chocolate company. The dashboard provides insights into revenue, profit, product performance, brand performance, country-wise sales, and monthly trends, helping stakeholders make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing products, brands, and countries.
- Monitor monthly revenue and profit trends.
- Compare category-wise sales and profitability.
- Provide interactive filtering for better business analysis.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### **Page 1 – Sales Performance Dashboard**
This page focuses on sales performance and revenue analysis.

#### KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Top Product Revenue
- Average Revenue Per Unit
- Revenue Per Customer

#### Visualizations
- Total Revenue by Month
- Total Revenue by Category
- Total Revenue by Country
- Brand-wise Revenue Analysis
- Interactive Product & Year Filters

---

### **Page 2 – Profitability Dashboard**
This page focuses on business profitability.

#### KPIs
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Top Product Profit
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth

#### Visualizations
- Total Profit by Month
- Total Profit by Category
- Total Profit by Country
- Brand-wise Profit Analysis
- Interactive Product & Year Filters

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Identified the highest revenue-generating product category.
- Compared profitability across different product categories.
- Analyzed country-wise revenue and profit contribution.
- Evaluated monthly sales and profit trends.
- Compared brand performance using revenue and profitability metrics.
